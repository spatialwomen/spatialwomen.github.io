Events

Nov. 2022

Women @ ACM SIGSPATIAL '22

A first networking event for female graduate students at ACM SIGSPATIAL '22. We gathered at the public lounge at the Seattle Marriott Waterfront hotel and shared our experiences as female researchers from various computer science and geospatial communities.

Mar. 2023

Invited Talk @ Thomas Jefferson Science and Technology High School

We gave a talk entitled "A Roadmap to Becoming a Computer Scientist" to an audience of 30 individuals at the Computer Club.

Apr. 2023

Networking session for underrepresented groups @ SIAM SDM '23

We organized a unique networking event aimed at promoting diversity in computer science. Diane Oyen from Los Alamos National Laboratory gave a invited talk and shared her insights on career development, including their experiences, challenges, and strategies for success. We had the opportunity to connect with researchers from diverse backgrounds and build meaningful professional relationships while supporting diversity in the field.
